Teen Mom fans call Farrah Abrahams mom Debra off the rails after she dons leather and fake

FORMER Teen Mom OG star Farrah Abraham's mom Debra shared a sneak peek at her upcoming rap song.

She captioned the post: "My new single 'White Woman’s Rap' is coming out this Friday!

"We are looking to shoot the music video later in summer; check out some dance routines you can expect to see.

"We’re also going to have a fun TikTok competition so check it out! #debradanielsen #debzog #dance #rap #funtimes #tiktok #tiktokdance #tiktokdances."
