Where is the last Odins Raven God of War Ragnarok?

1. Where is the last of Odin’s ravens in Helheim located?

The last of Odin’s ravens in Helheim is located in Helgrind, on the border to Shipyard of the Fallen. It is found on the way out of the area during the quest step, Reunion. It is above the Lore Marker found through another locked gate and wheel puzzle that requires Atreus’ Sigil arrows.

2. What is the hardest Raven to find in God of War Ragnarok?

The hardest Raven to find in God of War Ragnarok is the last Raven in Alfheim. You need to destroy the rubble under the outside entrance to the elven library first to unearth a crawlspace and locate it. You’ll find the Raven flying around the left-hand side after entering the crawlspace.

3. Are there more than 48 Ravens in Ragnarok?

No, there are a total of 48 ravens to find across Alfheim, Helheim, Midgard, Muspelheim, and Svartalfheim.

4. Where is the last eye of Odin?

The last Eye of Odin in The River Pass can be found in the hidden chamber below the Witch’s house. Ride the elevator down, and enter the arena and turn right, the bird is up high on a statue.

5. What happens if you get all the Ravens in God of War Ragnarok?

Once players find all ravens in God of War: Ragnarok, they gain a bundle of rewards. Upon each visit to the Raven Tree in Niflheim, the ravens will tell players deeper lore about Odin and the Raven Keeper.

6. God of War Ragnarok – All 48 Odin’s Ravens Locations

There are a total of 48 Odin’s Ravens to find in various locations throughout God of War Ragnarok. Here is a list of their locations:

1. Helgrind – Odin’s Raven 2/2
2. Jarnsmida Pitmines – Odin’s Raven 13/13
3. Niflheim – Odin’s Raven rewards from the Legendary Chests
4. Alfheim – The Applecore raven
5. Vanaheim – The Barrens raven 2
6. Midgard – River Delta Raven 2
7. Vanaheim – Surtr’s Forge Raven
8. Burning Cliffs – Odin’s Raven 1

7. Where is the 48th Raven?

The 48th Raven is found flying over a chasm next to a Wind Fissure in Helgrind.

8. Where is the last Odin’s Raven in Svartalfheim?

The last Raven in Svartalfheim is found at the entrance of Jarnsmida Pitmines.

9. Which of Odin’s eyes is missing?

In some myths, Odin’s one eye remains in Mímir’s well. But which eye is Odin missing? There is no consensus in the myths, and artistic depictions of Odin are not universal in their depiction of him.

10. Can you miss Ravens in God of War?

No, none of the Ravens are missable in God of War. Every single one of them can be collected after you’ve finished with the game.

11. What is the reward for killing Odin’s Ravens?

If you kill enough ravens, you will unlock a legendary chest at the base of The Raven Tree in Niflheim. These chests contain armor pieces or Runic attacks.

12. Are Odins Ravens missable?

No, none of the Ravens are missable in God of War. Once you’ve collected all of Odin’s Ravens, head back to Niflheim for a reward and a special fight.

13. Was Tyr always Odin?

No, Tyr was not always Odin. In Norse mythology, Tyr and Odin are two separate gods. However, there is a theory that suggests that Odin posed as Tyr in God of War Ragnarok to infiltrate Kratos’ circle of allies and know their plans.

14. What is the best armor in God of War Ragnarök?

The best armor in God of War Ragnarok is Surtr’s Scorched set. It provides huge stat bonuses to Strength, Defense, and Vitality, not to mention a couple of incredibly useful skills. However, it is also one of the most difficult and time-consuming sets to unlock.
