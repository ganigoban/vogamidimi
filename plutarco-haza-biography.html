BIOGRAPHY
Plutarco Haza is a Mexican actor, born in Ciudad Obregón, Sonora, on June 22, 1972. He spent part of his youth in Mazatlán, Sinaloa, where he became interested in theater and acting, which led him to settle in the city. from Mexico, to prepare at La Casa del Teatro, in the south of the city. Shortly before finishing her degree in theater, in 1996, Haza debuted in feature films, with a small role in the film En el aire (1995) and in the soap opera Si Dios me que la vida (1995), but it would be two years later. when he would achieve enormous recognition for his role as Andrés, in the popular telenovela Mirada de mujer (1997), establishing himself as one of the best talents of his generation. Among her career, Haza has worked on feature films such as A Wonderful World (2006), Here Between Us (2011), Melancolía (2021) and series such as Las Aparicio (2010), The Lord of the Skies (2015-2019) and The Secret by Selena (2019), among others.
