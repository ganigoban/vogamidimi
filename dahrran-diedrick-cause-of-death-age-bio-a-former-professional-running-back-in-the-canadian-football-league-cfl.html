Dahrran Diedrick Bio

Dahrran Diedrick played running back for the Canadian Football League (CFL) from January 11, 1979, to June 24, 2023. He won the Grey Cup three times, in 2005 with the Edmonton Eskimos and in 2009 and 2010 with the Montreal Alouettes.

He played for the Alouettes for the majority of his ten seasons in the Canadian Football League. He also played for the San Diego Chargers, Green Bay Packers, and Washington Redskins in the National Football League.
