Jennifer Aniston And Courteney Cox's Reunion Has Fans In A Tizzy

Is it just us, or are Courteney Cox and Jennifer Aniston the cutest BFFs ever? The "Friends" stars frequently make appearances in one another's Instagram posts, and when they do, fans can't get enough. On April 18, the two ladies joined forces on social media for a good cause. Aniston and Cox shared a boomerang clip of themselves side by side, sporting tees that read "Friends Forever" in the iconic "Friends" title font. Aniston tagged non-profit charities Americares and EBMRF, and while directed followers to the "Friends" Limited Edition Cast Collection site, a merch store with different apparel celebrating unforgettable moments from the show.

While some fans applauded the two women for using their platform to do good, others couldn't help but comment on the reunion. "I love you guys so much," one follower gushed. "I love when the cast does this, i'm waiting all the cast post," one fan commented. "Omg, look at you?! two gorgeous best friends," another chimed in. 

As most know, the "Friends" cast reunited for a special in 2021. Aniston shared a few posts to promote the special, and appeared to be on cloud nine following the special episode with her former castmates. "Still basking in all the love from the #FriendsReunion. Thank you each and every one of you," Aniston wrote. Best cast — and friends — ever!
