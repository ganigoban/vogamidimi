Coach Leah Patent Leather Loafer

If I was rebuilding my shoe collection the first pair I would start with is a classic pair of loafers. They are super versatile and can be dressed up or down and these Coach Leah Patent Leather Loafers would be the first pair I’d reach for. They come in matte and patent leather black, silver metallic, and green, plus sizes 5 through 11. It features a chunky rubber outsole that offers great traction, a lightweight 2.5-inch heel, and a plush memory foam insole for all-day comfort. 

Levi's Ribcage Straight-Ankle Jeans

The easiest way to elevate your closet is with quality pieces that you know you’ll get a ton of wear out of — especially those you can buy in multiple washes (because they go with everything). Don’t know what pair to start with? I’d go with these Levi's Ribcage Straight-Ankle Jeans that will style effortlessly for spring. They come in a range of distress levels and washes including medium, dark, and black, plus sizes 20 through 24 plus. It features a high-rise design and a close fit at the thigh, opening up to a wide leg at the mid-calf. They’re made of 99 percent cotton, making them soft on the skin, and the left over one percent offers a bit of stretch for a body-hugging fit. 

Trendy Long-Sleeve Queen Turtleneck

Nothing makes me feel more put together than a classic turtleneck, and the fit and cut of this Trendy Long-Sleeve Queen Turtleneck would elevate any outfit. It comes in 15 colors, including blue, pink, orange, black, and white in sizes S through XL. It has a close-to-body fit with lots of  stretch that hugs the body effortlessly and falls right at the hip. There's a classic fold-over collar that can also be styled scrunched, and the fabric is soft and lightweight, like your favorite tee. I’d pair it with jeans or trousers and kitten heeled flats.
