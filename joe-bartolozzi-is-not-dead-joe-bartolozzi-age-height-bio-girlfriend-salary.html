Joe Bartolozzi Career

Bartolozzi found his creative mind at a very young age while studying in an elementary school. He learned his craft from watching TV shows and videos.

He began his content creation journey on the Musical.ly app (now TikTok) and gradually expanded to other platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and Twitch. Since then his videos have included a wide range of aspects from his life, from daily activities to routine day-to-day essentials, Bartolozzi has managed to showcase a sense of relatability in his content.

Knowing that his fans enjoy his content and relate to his activities, excites and motivates him to give them his best.

Bartolozzi was always interested in sports. He mentions his earlier goal was to become a sportsman, but that was before content creation piqued his interest. He used to participate in sports events at his school when he wanted to pursue an athletic career.

His keen interest in sports has endured until now. He has been a part of several competitions in his school and college, while also winning several medals in high jump, relay, and hurdle race, among others. An Instagram post shows him with a bunch of medals from his sophomore year from June 2018.

Bartolozzi commenced his short-form video-making journey on the Musical.ly app. He gained a massive fan base in the app within a few months as his hilarious comedy videos went viral. After the property was acquired by ByteDance, his success grew even further on the now-changed TikTok app. He gained several millions of followers within a few months of posting lip-sync and comedic videos. With his rising fan base, Joe got recognition as one of the most trending TikTok stars in America.

Although he is currently known as a social media influencer and is more popular on TikTok, he enjoys the YouTube format too.

He currently uploads, gaming videos, travel vlogs, lifestyle posts, and excerpts of his Twitch streams.

He started his YouTube journey on Jun 2, 2020. He uploaded his debut video with the title “Why I Hate Public Bathrooms. Vibe Talks Episode 1” on Jul 7, 2020. His initial content involved gaming and lifestyle, which gained him lots of popularity.

His debut video gained a million views and thousands of likes. Some of his most popular videos are “Morgz Buys Fake Jewelry For Views?!?”, “Not The Among Us Meal….”, “The Worlds Hottest Chip…”, “This Game Made Me Cry..”, “People On TLC Can’t Be Real…”.
