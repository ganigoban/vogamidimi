Renee Paquette &amp; Jon Moxley Have Decided On Name For Unborn Daughter

Renee Paquette has confirmed the name of her and Jon Moxley’s unborn baby daughter. Paquette’s pregnancy announcement was made last November by her husband Jon Moxley during an episode of AEW Dynamite.

Renee Paquette previously worked as an interviewer and Raw commentator in WWE under the name of Renee Young. Her husband, Jon Moxley, competed in WWE from 2011 to 2019 as Dean Ambrose where he held the WWE Championship.

Renee Paquette was joined by her mother on the latest edition of her Oral Sessions podcast. During the podcast, she revealed that she and Jon Moxley will name their daughter Nora. This is a tribute to Paquette’s grandmother who’s name is Eleanor.

Jon Moxley and Renee Paquette have been married since 2017. Their daughter will be their first child. Moxley regualry competes on AEW Dynamite where he is a former AEW World Champion. At Double or Nothing, him and Eddie Kingston will face the Young Bucks for the AEW World Titles.
