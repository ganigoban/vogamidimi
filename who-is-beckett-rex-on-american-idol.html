Who is Beckett Rex on American Idol?

Who is Beckett Rex on American Idol? – Beckett Rex made a name for himself after quitting the prestigious music reality show, American Idol.

He is the son of the prominent entertainer, Malcolm McDowell. Malcolm McDowell is an accomplished actor and Golden Globe nominee with an impressive list of credits to his name. He is perhaps best known for his roles in popular films such as “If…”, “Time After Time”, “Rob Zombie’s Halloween” reboot and its sequel, “Easy A”, and “Bombshell”, among others.

Additionally, McDowell has also made his mark on the small screen, with starring roles on shows like “Franklin & Bash” and “Mozart in the Jungle”, as well as recurring roles on “Entourage” and “Heroes”. He is a proud parent of sons Finnian and Seamus, and also shares children Lily and Charlie with his former partner Steenburgen.

Recently, McDowell’s son, Rex, made headlines for his decision to quit a singing competition show in mid-April after making it to the Top 26. While the reason for his exit remains undisclosed, Rex had previously released original music including the singles “Wildfire” and “Pale Blue Eyes”.

In fact, he collaborated with Pierre Bouvier, the lead singer of Simple Plan, who happened to be one of his neighbors, and together they released an eight-song EP titled “Weirdo” in 2020.

Despite the disappointment of leaving the competition, Rex continues to make music and pursue his passion for singing. He has been in a relationship with Launa Fiorello-Taylor since September 2021. With his talent and determination, it will be exciting to see where his musical career takes him next.

Beckett Rex Social Media

As a young musician, Beckett Rex is significantly making use of the various social media platforms to promote his music.

On Instagram, he has a verified account with over 5,000 active followers and he hopes to grow the numbers as his popularity increases.
