Adele and Rumored Boyfriend Skepta Flirt on Instagram

  • Adele and her rumored boyfriend, U.K. artist Skepta, engaged in some flirty Instagram banter over the weekend.
  • "Finally got your Instagram password lol," wrote the rapper underneath a funny post from the singer rewatching her 2016 Glastonbury performance.
  • Adele responded to the comment with a winking emoji followed by a red heart, encouraging the relationship rumors that have surrounded the two musicians in recent months.

It all goes down in the comments section.

Adele and her speculated boyfriend, U.K. rapper Skepta, engaged in a bit of flirty social media banter over the weekend, per E! News. After the "Rolling in the Deep" singer took to Instagram to hilariously post pics of herself rewatching her own 2016 Glastonbury performance at home, Skepta left an intriguing, inside joke-esque comment on the post that alluded to the two keeping close communication lately.

"Finally got your Instagram password lol," commented Skepta. Fans were quick to point out the musician's seemingly intimate comment, as well as leave quips of their own: "Wait a damn minute!" and "Did you take the pic?" were left in response to the rapper's appearance underneath the post. Adele responded to Skepta's teasing, replying with a winking emoji followed by a red heart.

Instagram

Neither Skepta nor Adele has publicly acknowledged or confirmed a potential romance between the two, but Skepta admitted previously that the two have shared a close bond over the years.

"Adele texts me all the time and keeps me in check," the musician told ES Magazine in 2016 regarding their friendship.
