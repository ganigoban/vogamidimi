Fan Set To Get Nightmare Family Tattoo After Cody Rhodes Liked His Tweet

Cody Rhodes’ infamous neck tattoo has become his style statement over the years since its debut on AEW. Now a fan will have the same Nightmare Family tattoo as him because of prerequisite he tweeted.

It all started when a fan tweeted, “If Cody Rhodes likes this, I’ll tattoo his logo on my finger.” The American Nightmare of course liked the said tweet and now the fan has to live up to his words.

Fan confirmed that he is indeed getting the tattoo after the WWE Superstar liked his tweet. We’ll wait to see how that turns out.

“APPOINTMENT HAS BEEN MADE FOR NEXT THURSDAY WHEN IM OFF WORK! ✨👏🏻,” wrote fan

The fan later specified that he’ll get the design somewhere else if it doesn’t look good on his finger, but he’ll definitely get it done. “THIS AINT AN L! It’s A W! And i’m not pwussy!,” he tweeted. “And if my artist says it won’t look best on my finger then i’ll get it done somewhere on my hand/arm but it’ll get done lmao”

Cody Rhodes always appreciates such gestures from fans. Back in 2020, another fan got the American Nightmare tattoo inked on his neck and received massive praise from Rhodes himself.
