Sammy Hagar Net Worth

Sammy Hagar is an American singer and songwriter born on October 13, 1947 in Salinas, CA. He is best known for replacing David Lee Roth as the lead singer of the rock band Van Halen, and has been nicknamed The Red Rocker. In 2017, he began touring with the supergroup The Circle, and continues to be a popular figure in the music industry.
