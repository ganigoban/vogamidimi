Who is Michael Bolton?

Michael Bolton is a renowned American singer and songwriter, known for his powerful voice and emotive ballads. Born in New Haven, Connecticut, Bolton initially started his career in hard rock and heavy metal genres before transitioning to soft rock and pop in the mid-1980s.

His soulful voice and poignant lyrics have earned him a vast fan base and significant acclaim in the adult contemporary music scene. Bolton has won multiple Grammy Awards and has been celebrated for hits like “How Am I Supposed to Live Without You” and “When a Man Loves a Woman.”
