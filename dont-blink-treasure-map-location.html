God of War guide: Dont Blink treasure map locations

In this God of War guide, we’ll show you how to find the Don’t Blink treasure.

Where to find the Don’t Blink treasure map

Grid View

You can find the treasure map in Fafnir’s Storeroom during the “Fafnir’s Storeroom” Favor (or at any point thereafter, really). Atreus can pick it up next to the knight in the center of the hub room.

Editor’s note: We captured some of the images in the gallery below late into our God of War game. If you haven’t completed “The Sickness” Journey, then it may contain things that you don’t want to see yet. The text, though, is spoiler-free.

Don’t Blink treasure location

The Don’t Blink treasure map’s clue reads:

My journey will be long and I can only carry the essentials. I leave my possessions under the watchful eyes of the face of stone until I return.

Grid View

The clue leads you to the Mountain. Here’s how to find the Don’t Blink buried treasure:

  • Head to the base of the mountain. You’re looking for the entrance that’s carved to look like a head. (That’s the “watchful eyes of the face of stone” that the clue refers to.)
  • Look to your left as you approach the entrance. The treasure is before the last few starts that lead there, on the cliff’s edge. (See the gallery above for the precise location.)

    • Unearth the treasure to get the Glacial Catalyst crafting item and the Eye of Fury Legendary enchantment. Browse the gallery above for the details.
