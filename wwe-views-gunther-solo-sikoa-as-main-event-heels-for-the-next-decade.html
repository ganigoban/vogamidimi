WWE Views GUNTHER &amp; Solo Sikoa As Main Event Heels For The Next Decade

WWE has some great potential on the roster right now. That being said, some people really stand out as Superstars that they can really push for years to come.

Solo Sikoa is the youngest member of the Bloodline, but he is also a force to be reckoned with. Also, GUNTHER is IC Champion, and his current reign is poised to be record-breaking, with no sign of that run coming to an end.

While speaking to Give Me Sport, Wrestle Votes said that WWE feels as though they have bona fide main event heels for the next decade with GUNTHER and Solo Sikoa.

“WWE feel like they have bona fide main event heels for the next 10 years on the roster already, and they’re going to treat them as such. I’m sure you can figure those two guys out, but the impression so far that GUNTHER and Solo Sikoa have made are putting those two guys at the front of the line for the next 10 years.”

WWE is always making big plans for the future, and it would be no surprise to see Solo Sikoa and GUNTHER in great spots for years to come. If anything, their current booking trajectory would certainly put them in that tier of the roster.

We will have to see what the WWE roster looks like in a decade. Solo Sikoa is certainly getting off to a great start, and at 30-years-old, he could do a lot in WWE. Also, GUNTHER 36-years-old, GUNTHER is still in the prime of his career.
