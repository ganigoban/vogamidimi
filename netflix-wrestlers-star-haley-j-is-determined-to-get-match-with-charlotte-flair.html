Netflix Wrestlers Star Haley J Is Determined To Get Match With Charlotte Flair

WWE held some tryouts recently, and they had some notable names in attendance. You can click here for full list of people who showed up. Sadly, Hollyhood Haley J couldn’t make it.

Hollyhood Haley J was supposed to get WWE tryout, but that didn’t work. She had paperwork issues, so that didn’t happen this time around. She’s not going to let that get her down.

Haley J got on X, and she made it very clear that even though she missed this WWE tryout, it doesn’t mean she’s giving up on her dreams. She wants a match against Charlotte Flair, and Hollyhood is determined to make that happen!

“Don’t get it twisted I’m still going to be coming for my match with @MsCharlotteWWE may not be now but.. That’s what I want. That’s who I want. & I GET WHAT I WANT. STAY TUNED.”

Hollywood Haley J is featured on Netflix’s “Wrestlers” series about OVW. It’s a great watch, and any fan of pro wrestling should give it a try, especially if they like Al Snow.

That Netflix docuseries about OVW also drummed up some controversy with James Storm, but The Cowboy squashed that as best he could. The sky seems to be the limit for Hollyhood Haley J though, as she waits for her next opportunity.
