Tokyo Sexwale Net Worth

Tokyo Sexwale is a South African activist, politician, and entrepreneur who was born on March 5, 1953. He is best known for his fight against apartheid, for which he served time in prison on Robben Island. After his release, he went on to serve as the Premier of Gauteng Province and the Minister of Human Settlements in the South African government.
