Dominik Mysterio Wants His Parents Arrested After Christmas Fiasco

Dominik Mysterio shocked fans when he turned heel by attacking Edge and then his father Rey Mysterio few months ago. Dominik truly came into his own as a heel over the past few months, as fans truly despise him now. Dominik even attacked his father on Thanksgiving after that. Mysterio and Rhea Ripley decided to cause chaos at Rey Mysterio’s place on Christmas Eve, but it ended with Dominik getting arrested.

Dominik Mysterio and Rhea Ripley wanted to ruin Rey Mysterio’s holiday spirits on Christmas Eve. However, Mysterio called the police and had his son arrested, while Rhea Ripley got slapped by Dominik’s mother.

The Judgement Day member took to Twitter and revealed that he was finally out of jail. Mysterio also added a hashtag, where he called for his parents Rey and Angie Mysterio to get arrested.
