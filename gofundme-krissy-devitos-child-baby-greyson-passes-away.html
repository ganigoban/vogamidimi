GoFundMe; Krissy Devitos Child Baby Greyson Passes Away

On Thursday, a head-on collision accident involving a guy Krissy Devito , a woman, and five kids occurred in Lauderdale County.

Emma Wade, a 63-year-old Lexington resident who was operating the car, perished in the collision, according to Lauderdale County Coroner Kim Jones. Wade and the five kids in the 2010 Honda Pilot were all flown to Vanderbilt Medical Center.

The other car was a 2021 Freightliner tractor-trailer operated by Dennis Miller, 55, of Cullman, according to ALEA. Miller was taken for medical attention at North Alabama Medical Center after suffering an injury.

Around 9:35 a.m., a collision occurred on Highway 64 close to Lauderdale County 381, 5 miles east of Lexington. Investigators from ALEA are looking into the collision.
