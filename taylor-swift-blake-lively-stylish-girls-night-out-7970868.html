Taylor Swift and Blake Lively Stepped Out for a Stylish Girls' Night

Ever since her breakup from Joe Alwyn earlier this year, Taylor Swift has been spending some much-needed quality time with her girlfriends. Back in April, she brought her girl gang back together for a bestie dinner in New York City, and a few months after that, she hosted her first Fourth of July party in seven years, where everyone from BFF Selena Gomez and all three members of Haim were in attendance. 

Meanwhile, just last night, Taylor had a girls' night out with longtime squad member Blake Lively at Zero Bond in Manhattan's Noho neighborhood. During the outing, each showcased their distinct personal style, with Taylor going schoolcore chic in a charcoal gray pleated miniskirt, a black cardigan, and maroon leather knee-high boots, while Blake stuck with her signature style move and mixed multiple patterns. The actress paired a denim shirt that featured a colorful patchwork lining with a printed T-shirt, a tweed miniskirt, and tartan pumps. 

Their outfits might've been polar opposite, but Blake and Taylor's glam was totally in synch. Both girls wore their long, blonde hair down in loose, beach-y waves while also sporting a bold red lip. Taylor added a swipe of winged liner to her beauty look, while Blake, on the other hand, opted for bronzy eyeshadow instead. 

Earlier in the day, Swift was seemingly hanging out with her other BFF, Selena Gomez. Over on Instagram, Selena shared a set of images of the besties posing for selfies together while dining outdoors.
