Dennis Haysbert Net Worth

Dennis Haysbert is a well-known actor, born on June 2, 1954 in San Mateo, CA. He is best known for his roles as the U.S. President on the television series 24 and as Sergeant Jonas Blane on The Unit. He also played the role of Pedro Cerrano in the Major League film franchise and voiced the character General Hologram in the 2012 Disney feature Wreck-It Ralph.
