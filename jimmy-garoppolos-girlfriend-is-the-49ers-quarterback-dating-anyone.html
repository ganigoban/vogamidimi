Is The 49ers' Quarterback Dating Anyone?

In addition to being spotted out on a date with adult film star Kiara Maria in 2018, there was another woman Jimmy Garoppolo made headlines with that year. According to the New York Post, the San Francisco 49ers quarterback dated model Alexandra King from about February to April 2018, although he never confirmed it himself. "It was news to me," he told Bleacher Report, after reading about his alleged romance with King.

Even so, TMZ snagged photos of the pair holding hands at Disneyland, and King reportedly shared a pic of Garoppolo on Valentine's Day. However, things might not have ended the best between these two, considering King allegedly posted an Instagram Story on a since-deleted IG account that read, "Karma" along with a smiley face, when Garoppolo was carted off the field with an injury during a game in September 2018, per the Post. Yikes! Maybe that was just a coincidence. We'll let you decide that one, too.
