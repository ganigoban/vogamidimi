Jennifer Lopez Reportedly Hates Afflecks Smoking Habit: Insiders

Apparently, months later, Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck are allegedly fighting over the same thing: his habits. While Affleck has given up many vices and has gone through great strides to give them up, like with his alcohol dependency. But his smoking? While he’s also made great strides with that, Lopez allegedly still wants him to quit it ASAP.

Close sources told Life and Style Magazine that she “hates” this habit so much, and apparently, she’s felt this way for months.
