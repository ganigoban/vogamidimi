21 Savage’s Music Career

21 Savage’s music career is the primary source of his wealth. He made his debut in 2015 with his mixtape “The Slaughter Tape.” Since then, he has released two studio albums, both of which have been certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).

Album Sales

21 Savage’s debut studio album, “Issa Album,” was released in 2017 and debuted at number two on the Billboard 200. His second studio album, “I Am > I Was,” debuted at number one on the Billboard 200. The sales from these albums significantly contribute to his net worth.

Streaming Revenue

With the rise of digital platforms, 21 Savage has also earned substantial income from streaming services. His songs have amassed billions of streams on platforms like Spotify and Apple Music, generating significant revenue.

Concerts and Tours

Another significant source of 21 Savage’s income is his concerts and tours. He has performed at numerous music festivals and has embarked on several successful tours, both as a headliner and as a supporting act.

Live Performances

21 Savage’s live performances are a significant part of his income. He has performed at some of the biggest music festivals, including Coachella and Rolling Loud, earning substantial fees for his appearances.

Tour Revenue

21 Savage’s tours have also contributed to his wealth. His “Issa Tour” in 2017 and “I Am > I Was Tour” in 2019 were both successful, with sold-out shows across the United States.

Endorsements and Partnerships

21 Savage has also earned income through various endorsements and partnerships. He has collaborated with several brands, including fashion and lifestyle companies.

Brand Endorsements

Partnerships

Real Estate Investments

Philanthropy

Conclusion

21 Savage’s net worth in 2024 is projected to be around $25 million, a testament to his successful career in the music industry.
