Jesse Owens Net Worth

Jesse Owens was an African-American track star who was born on September 12, 1913 in Oakville, AL. He achieved great success in his career, setting three world records in less than an hour while competing for Ohio State University in 1935, and then going on to win four gold medals at the 1936 Berlin Olympics in the 100m, 200m, 4x100m, and long jump. His accomplishments are still remembered today as a testament to his incredible talent and determination.
