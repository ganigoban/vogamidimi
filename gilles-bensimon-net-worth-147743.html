Gilles Bensimon Net Worth

Gilles Bensimon is a French photographer who was born on February 29, 1944. He is best known for his work as the creative director of Elle Magazine, where he began his career with the French edition and later went on to photograph internationally renowned models such as Tyra Banks and Naomi Campbell for the American edition.
