Liv Morgan Qualifies for Womens Elimination Chamber Match on 2/12 WWE RAW

The WWE women’s division is gearing up for a huge opportunity in the Elimination Chamber in Perth, Australia with Liv Morgan now joining them inside the deadly structure following her victory on RAW tonight.

Liv Morgan went into the match, as the first stop of her Revenge tour that she has openly stated is against the current WWE Women’s World champion, Rhea Ripley. Zoey Stark, on the other hand, wanted one more dance against Mami and wanted to earn that chance by being a part of the Chamber match.

The match between two of the top RAW stars of the women’s division with high-stakes implications was an enthralling sight for the WWE Universe. In the end, Morgan countered a corkscrew move by Stark from the top rope and hit her with the Oblivion to secure the pinfall.

With this win, she now joins Bianca Belair and Becky Lynch in the Women’s Elimination Chamber match in less than two weeks. The winner of the match would either challenge Rhea Ripley or Nia Jax for the Women’s World title.

For Liv, this could be her opportunity to win the Chamber match and reach the final destination of her Revenge tour against Ripley, the same woman that cost six months of her wrestling career, provided she retains her title against Jax on the same night.
