Gary Levox Net Worth

Gary Levox was born on July 10, 1970 in Columbus, OH and is best known as the lead vocalist for the country music trio Rascal Flatts. The group has had an impressive career, with more than twenty singles on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart. Levox has been a part of the group since its formation in 1999 and continues to be a major part of their success.
